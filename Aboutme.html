About Me

UX Designer

I'm Dochianu Ionut, aspiring user experience designer. I place a lot of value on inclusivity, thus I created a lot of user-friendly designs for various target audiences, from designing new user interfaces for already-existing firms to creating a brand-new creative tool for others. I recently completed a minor in Game Development and Simulation while studying User Experience Design at The Hague University of Applied Sciences, which gave me new ideas about how to utilize design to convey a story. I believe that design should serve as a bridge between people and their distinctive modes of expression. Especially when working in a team and the result can be considered as a love letter, I adore vibrant and eye-catching designs.
